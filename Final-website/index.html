The School District of Philadelphia offers over 300 hundred different schools. We look to give parents and caretakers in Philadelphia the information they need to select the best school for their child. The current tools provide a series of detailed dashboards for each individual school that make it difficult to compare and contrast schools simulataneously, which hinders parents' ability to efficiently analyze their options.
